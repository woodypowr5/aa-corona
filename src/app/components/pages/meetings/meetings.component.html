Notes

Want to host a meeting?

Contact Chris at reddingrecoveryonline@outlook.com. No meetings will be held during times at which regularly scheduled AA meeting are held in the Redding area, and are still active given the current COVID-19 situation. See the Redding AA Norcal Schedule here: https://www.aanorcal.org/

Concerned about anonymity?

Zoom meetings are launched with both audio and video disabled by default. So don't worry about inadvertently compromising your anonymity. Feel free to enable video or audio when you wish, or not at all. Feel free to just listen. I'm not sure about Google Hangouts.

Don't have a computer?

Or don't want to download the app? You can also call in from a phone. Click on the link for the meeting you want to join, and there will be a call-in phone number and a meeting ID. Just call the number and enter the meeting ID when prompted.
